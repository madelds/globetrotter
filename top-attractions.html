Two Lovers Point

Two Lovers Point, or Puntan Dos Amantes, is a dramatic cliffside lookout point that offers breathtaking panoramic views of the Pacific Ocean. According to Chamorro legend, it's named after two lovers who jumped from the cliff to be together forever. Visitors can enjoy the stunning vistas, stroll along scenic pathways, and learn about the romantic folklore surrounding the site.

Chamorro Village

Chamorro Village, also known as Wednesday Night Market, is a cultural hub in Guam where visitors can experience the vibrant spirit of the local Chamorro community. Every Wednesday evening, the village comes alive with food stalls offering traditional Chamorro cuisine, local crafts, live music, and dance performances. It's a fantastic opportunity to immerse yourself in Guam's rich cultural heritage and enjoy the lively atmosphere of the island.

Ypao Beach

Ypao Beach is one of the most popular and picturesque beaches in Guam. With its soft white sand, crystal-clear turquoise waters, and stunning sunsets, it's an ideal spot for sunbathing, swimming, snorkeling, and water sports. The beach is lined with resorts, restaurants, and shops, making it a convenient and vibrant destination for visitors.
