Proa Restaurant

Address: 210 Guerrero St, Hagatna, Guam

Proa Restaurant is a popular dining destination in Guam, offering a fusion of Chamorro, Pacific, and American cuisines. Located in Hagatna, the capital city of Guam, Proa is known for its flavorful dishes, vibrant atmosphere, and waterfront views. It's a must-visit for food enthusiasts looking to experience the diverse culinary scene of Guam.

Pika's Cafe

Address:1300 Pale San Vitores Road, Tumon, Guam

Pika's Cafe in Guam is a popular dining destination known for its vibrant atmosphere and diverse menu offerings. Situated in the heart of Tumon, it offers a unique fusion of Chamorro and international cuisine, showcasing the island's rich culinary heritage. From traditional Chamorro dishes to innovative creations, Pika's Cafe delights visitors with its flavorful and fresh ingredients, making it a must-visit spot for both locals and tourists seeking an authentic taste of Guam.

Meskla Chamoru Fusion Bistro

Address: 156 Pale San Vitores Rd, Tumon, Guam

Meskla Chamoru Fusion Bistro is renowned for its creative fusion of Chamorro and international cuisines. Situated in Tumon, one of Guam's main tourist districts, Meskla offers a unique dining experience with its innovative dishes and relaxed ambiance. Whether you're craving traditional Chamorro flavors or global culinary delights, Meskla has something to satisfy every palate.
